Swimming Tips

How to kick your feet

A lot of swimmers, especially those starting out tend to focus on their arms and neglect what they are supposed to be doing with their feet. This tip focuses on how to learn to kick your feet during the freestyle stroke.

  1. Find a good kickboard that you can use. This will allow you to focus on just your kicking technique.
  2. As you get in the pool hold the board in your hands and pratice a scissor-like kick with your feet down the pool
