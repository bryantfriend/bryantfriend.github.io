Swimming

Swimming has long been an activity humans have been doing since there was water. There are many reasons why people swim. It could be recreational to have fun with friends or even for work. Some people dive for pearls or swim to find clams or other food. Through this site you will be able to find out all sorts of information about one of my favorite hobbies: swimming. To begin it's important to understand all the different types of swimming people do. On this page you will see a basic description of the various strokes and even a picture. On some of the menu items up top you can view these strokes in action in various races or even learn how to perfrom these strokes in the Swimming Tips. I really hope that you will be able to learn about swimming here and start enjoying one of lifes most tranguill activites.
